My Favorite Animal is a Monkey

Celebes crested macaque

The Celebes crested macaque is a striking primate native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi and nearby smaller islands. It is easily recognized by its jet-black fur, long face, and a distinctive tuft of hair or crest on top of the head. Their golden-brown eyes stand out sharply against their dark skin and fur.
